1993 Mitsubishi Pajero Field Guard concept car

August 2021

The Mitsubishi Pajero Field Guard is an obscure 90s concept car inteded for lifeguards (It could float). I find the design of this car extremly of its 90s/early 2000s era, it could be even argued that today it looks retrofuturistic. It looks like nothing else. Since I couldn't find any models/renderings online, I've made one and here it is. It's not 100% accurate because as far as I know there are no schematics available, I had to use a couple of blurry magazine photos and screenshots from a Jackie Chan movie as reference.

Summary

Hello, my name is Adam, I'm a computer science student and a 3D artist living in Poland. I've always been passionate about art, technology and 2D/3D design. I would judge my level of experience as intermediate, I’ve been learning 3D for 2 years now. Currently I try to focus on modelling and texturing in Blender but I can also do animations. I started doing freelance work on Fiverr last year. Feel free to contact me there for a commission or even better write me an email. I'm sure we'll work something out.

Key abilities

I am quite well versed in Blender, my strong points are hard surface modelling and environment design. I have no problems with learning new techniques and software, I can easily and quickly adapt to the requirements of a particular project. I never miss deadlines and work fast. You will have no problems communicating with me or working out project details. I’m fluent in English and respond to all messages.

Software proficiency

Blender is the main piece of 3D software I currently use for modelling, animation, texturing and rendering. Recently I started learning Substance Painter to better my texturing skills. When it comes to image editing, I use Gimp or Krita for raster graphics and Open Toonz for vector graphics and animation. For video editing I mainly use Davinci Resolve.

For a commission it’s best if you contact me directly via email but if you prefer you can find me on Fiverr too.

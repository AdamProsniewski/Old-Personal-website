1993 Mitsubishi Pajero Field Guard concept car

20.05.2020

The Mitsubishi Pajero Field Guard is an obscure 90s concept car inteded for lifeguards (It could float). I find the design of this car extremly of its 90s/early 2000s era, it could be even argued that today it looks retrofuturistic. It looks like nothing else. Since I couldn't find any models/renderings online, I've made one and here it is. It's not 100% accurate because as far as I know there are no schematics available, I had to use a couple of blurry magazine photos and screenshots from a Jackie Chan movie as reference.
